About CSP Training Academy

CSP Academy aims to close the skills gap in rural and semi-urban communities, providing quality education and training for both working and non-working individuals. We're committed to empowering our students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in today's competitive job market.

Our Courses

We offer a comprehensive range of short-term and long-term courses designed to equip you with practical skills for the modern workplace.

Business Communication

Learn effective communication strategies for sharing information within and outside your organization for commercial benefit. Master professional writing, presentation skills, and interpersonal communication.

Project Administration

Develop skills in organizing team members, facilitating project workflows, reporting, and analyzing projects under the supervision of a project manager. Perfect for aspiring project coordinators.

Computer Training

Comprehensive training in software, hardware, networking, programming, and database management. From basic computer literacy to advanced technical skills, we cover it all.
